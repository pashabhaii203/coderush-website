About Code Rush

Code Rush is an exciting coding competition as part of Technovision 2025.

Code Rush is more than just a competition—it's a battlefield for coders to showcase their skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities. As part of Technovision 2025, this event challenges participants with puzzles, problems, and logical reasoning tasks in C, C++, and Python.
Multiple Rounds – The competition consists of multiple rounds, with each round increasing in difficulty.
Problem Solving – Participants solve coding challenges ,be the one who Win the price and get certificate too .
Languages Allowed – You can code in C, C++, or Python as per your preference.
Mark The Date Event Date:5th April 2025
Venue: Tecnia institue of advance studies
Time: 11:30 AM onwards. The competition will take place in Lab 2, PG Building.

Guidelines

Individual participation
Registration fees is 50/-
Duration is 1 hr.
Tech stack:c,c++,python etc.
Problems increase in difficulty
Points awarded for correctness and efficiency
Judging criteria: Accuracy, execution time, and optimal solutions

Why To Participate?

1. Boost your coding skills, challenge yourself, and compete with the best minds!
2. Enhance problem-solving abilities, improve logical thinking, and prepare for coding interviews.
3. Get recognition, earn certificates, and take a step towards your dream career in tech!
4. Push your limits and experience the thrill of competitive coding!
